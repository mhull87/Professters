Acquire an Instructor, Professter, or Credibytes (CB) by purchase or participating in contests or giveaways. A Professter or membership may be awarded for sponsorships and partnerships. Earn classroom credits by completing various challenges set forth by our official task force. Challenges may also include bonus awards such as an Instructor or Professter NFT, Credibytes, or merchandise.

You will be able to exchange your Instructor, Professter, or Credibytes for various offers, upgrades, prizes, merchandise, and more. You can share and exchange Credibytes with friends too. Complete a Master's set of Credibytes to exchange for a Professter or membership upgrades. You may exchange one or more completed Master's sets to acquire higher degrees of rarity as well as advance to the next tier of membership.

To access the network, connect your wallet to authenticate and verify ownership. Once you are verified, you can then create an account. There are five tiers of membership. Each tier provides varying degrees of access, benefits, offers, and services.
