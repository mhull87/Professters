Become a member

Acquire an Instructor, Professter, or Credibytes (CB) by donating at least $5 or participating in contests or giveaways. A Professter or membership may be awarded for sponsorships and partnerships. Anyone can earn an Instructor, Professter, or Credibytes by completing various challenges set forth by our official task force.

You will be able to exchange your Instructor, Professter, or Credibytes for various offers, upgrades, prizes, merchandise, and more. Complete a Master's set of Credibytes to exchange for a Professter or membership upgrades. You may exchange one or more completed Master's sets to acquire higher degrees of rarity as well as advance to the next tier of membership.

To access the network, connect your wallet to authenticate and verify ownership. Once you are verified, you can then create an account. There are five tiers of membership. Each tier provides varying degrees of access, benefits, offers, and services.

Tier 1:

Min. $5 donation or ownership of any one CB or GEN1 Instructors
Entry-level membership

Tier 2:

Min. $100 donation or GEN1 ownership + $100 CC Credits
Enhanced profile and account settings
Entry-level access to content, resources, materials, and merchandise
Entry-level access to events, grants, and funding opportunities

Tier 3:

Min. $1000 donation or GEN2 ownership + $500 CC credits
Verified membership and status upgrade
Max profile settings
Entry-level access to professional services
Enhanced access to content, resources, materials, and merchandise

Tier 4:

Min. $5000 donation or GEN2 (Epic) + $1500 CC credits
Enhanced access to professional services
Entry-level access to sponsorships and partnerships

Tier 5:

Min. $10,000 donation or GEN3 (Mythic) Professter
Full access to our organization, network, grants, professional services, events, promotions, content, and merchandise. Max level of sponsorship and partnership opportunities
